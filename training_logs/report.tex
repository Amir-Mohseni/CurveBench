\section*{Model Key}

All run names in the charts below use the following anonymized labels.
De-anonymized names will be provided after the review period.

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\toprule
\textbf{Label} & \textbf{Architecture} & \textbf{Reward} \\
\midrule
model-a & qwen3-vl-8b-tree-only  & tree isomorphism only (weight 1.0) \\
model-b & qwen3-vl-8b-tree-node  & tree (0.7) + node count (0.3) \\
model-c & gemma3-12b-rlvr        & tree (0.7) + node count (0.3) \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

Training used GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization) on CurveBench-Easy
(\texttt{total\_train}, 210 examples). Reward functions are deterministic and
verifiable: \textbf{tree\_reward} = 1.0 iff predicted tree is isomorphic to
ground truth; \textbf{node\_count\_reward} = 1.0 iff predicted node count matches
ground truth.
